Checkbox customization:
https://medium.com/@rjstech/styling-checkboxes-with-css-9c817bc049a0
- turn checkbox into circular shape

HTML:
    <div id="right-buttons">
        <input value="1" type="checkbox" id="mycheck" />
        <label id="checklabel" for="mycheck">
       </label>
    </div>

CSS: 
input[type="checkbox"]{
  visibility:hidden;
}

#checklabel{
  height:50px;
  width:50px;
  background-color:rgb(84, 255, 68);
  display:block;
  border-radius:100%;
}

input[type="checkbox"]:checked + label{
  border:2px solid rgb(4, 146, 4);
}
